Recent and past projects (somewhat chronological)

Quantum Memories

Quantum memories are a crucial part of quantum communication and quantum computation. I am doing my Masters thesis about quantum memories in atomic ensembles at the Photonic Quantum Information Group of Ian Walmsley at Imperial College London. So far it is a perfect combination of aspects of Quantum Optics and Atomic physics. Along with the pitfalls of experimental physics of course...

String theory

String theory is an illusive but nontheless interesting and fascinating subject. Here you will find some texts and references. However: "It doesn't matter how beautiful your theory is, it doesn't matter how smart you are. If it doesn't agree with experiment, it's wrong."

You can find my seminar thesis on torus amplitudes and modular invariance here. The bible for introductory string theory can be found here


Circuit Quantum Dynamics

Circuit Quantum Dynamics is the foundation for superconducting qubits, the most promising realisation of computable quantum bits. You can find an amateur bachelor project on the topic here.
