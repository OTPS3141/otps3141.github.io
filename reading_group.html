Reading group Ideals, Varieties and Algorithms

Together with Francesco Maria Mascarin I am organising a reading group on Ideals, Varieties and Algorithms by Cox, Little and O'Shea.

The reading group is open to all interested students and researchers. The aim is to read the book together, discuss the content and solve exercises.

In case of questions, please write a mail to Francesco or me

Textbook

  • Ideals, Varieties and Algorithms by Cox, Little and O'Shea (Fourth Edition), ISBN 978-3-319-16720-6
  • PDF version can be found here

Schedule

We will meet twice a week for total eight weeks. During the sessions on Tuesday we will discuss content, while on Friday we will discuss exercises that have been assigned on Tuesday.

The reading group will be held online, a Zoom link will be communicated via mail.

For each session, a session chair will be assigned, who will make a summary of the assigned chapters and lead the discussion.
